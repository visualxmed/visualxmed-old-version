The Executive Team

ALICE SHIEH
FOUNDER AND CEO

Alice attended the University of California, Berkeley before transferring to Duke University, where she graduated in 2015 with a Chemistry degree. A Bay Area girl through and through, she has since returned to California and is currently working as a research assistant in a cardiovascular lab at Stanford University. She has always been interested in the intersection of education, research, and medicine and eventually would like to pursue a career in academic medicine. In her free time, she enjoys swimming, dancing, and exploring new hiking trails.

GLORIA YE
FOUNDER AND CTO

Gloria was a former West Point cadet before attending UC Berkeley where she graduated in 2013. Currently, she is working in tech in Silicon Valley. In her spare time, she enjoys hitting up beaches with her English Golden Retriever, swimming, and discovering hidden treasures within various downtowns in the Bay Area.
